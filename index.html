About FieldVista

Who we are

Our approach

We start with the field and move to the model. We blend rigorous evaluation methods with practical diagnostics, geospatial data, and network analytics. Our goal is to produce decisions that are clear, testable, and ready for scale.

  • Evidence tied to program decisions
  • Simple tools teams can use
  • Transparent methods and reproducible code

Leadership

FieldVista is led by economists and policy researchers with deep experience in agriculture, social networks, and risk. The team has delivered RCTs, insurance redesigns, and M&E systems across Indian states and partners.
